About Learnable

Learnable is a revolutionary learning management system designed with accessibility at its core. We believe education should be available to everyone, regardless of their abilities or learning preferences.

Our platform integrates cutting-edge speech technology, keyboard navigation, and visual design principles to create an inclusive learning environment. Every feature is built with WCAG 2.1 AA compliance in mind, ensuring that students with visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive disabilities can fully participate in their education.
